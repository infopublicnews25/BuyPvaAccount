Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take? +
Most orders are processed and delivered within 5-15 minutes after payment confirmation. During peak hours, it may take up to 30 minutes.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cryptocurrency payments (BTC, ETH, USDT) and Cash on Delivery for local customers. All payments are processed securely.
What if I receive a suspended account? +
All our accounts are verified before delivery. If you receive a suspended account, contact our support team immediately for a replacement or refund.
Do you provide refunds? +
We offer refunds within 24 hours of purchase if the account is suspended or doesn't meet our quality standards. Refund requests must be submitted through our support system.
How do I access my purchased accounts? +
After your order is completed, you'll receive login credentials via email and can also access them through your account dashboard under the "Downloads" section.
