Summary: We collect only what we need to process orders, provide support, prevent abuse, and run the Site.

1) Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how BuyPvaAccount (the “Site”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit or use our website and services.

2) Information we collect

  • Order information: items purchased, order amounts, timestamps, and delivery status.
  • Contact information: details you provide for support and order updates (such as email/phone, if you provide them).
  • Support content: messages, attachments, and troubleshooting information you send in tickets.
  • Technical data: IP address, device/browser details, pages viewed, and approximate location derived from IP (for security and analytics).
  • Payment information: payment status and transaction references from third‑party payment providers. We do not store full card data; crypto transactions are processed via external providers and/or blockchain networks.

3) How we use information

  • To create, process, and deliver orders
  • To provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and prohibited activity
  • To maintain site security, stability, and performance
  • To comply with legal obligations and lawful requests

4) Cookies and local storage

We may use cookies and browser storage (such as localStorage) to remember your preferences, keep carts working, and improve user experience. You can clear cookies/storage in your browser settings, but some features may stop working.

5) Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when necessary:

  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, customer support tools, and payment processors (only the data needed for their role).
  • Legal/compliance: when required by law, court order, or lawful government request.
  • Security: to investigate suspected fraud, abuse, or threats to users or the Site.

6) Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for order delivery, support, accounting, security, and legal compliance. Retention periods vary depending on the data type and purpose.

7) Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect data. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security. Please keep your devices and communications secure.

8) International visitors

If you access the Site from outside our operating country/region, your information may be processed in locations where we or our providers operate, and data protection laws may differ.

9) Your choices

  • You can request correction of inaccurate information you provided to us.
  • You can request deletion of certain data, subject to legal, accounting, and security requirements.
  • You can opt out of non‑essential cookies where applicable via browser settings.

10) Children’s privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date shows when changes were made. Continued use of the Site means you accept the updated policy.

12) Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact support using the Site support/ticket options or email if provided on the Site.
